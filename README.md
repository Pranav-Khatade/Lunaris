#🌙 Lunaris

A 2D RPG survival game set on the harsh and mysterious surface of the Moon. Built with Godot Engine.

#📝 Game Overview

Lunaris puts you in the suit of a stranded astronaut who must mine resources, trade with a lunar shopkeeper, upgrade equipment, and rebuild a broken spaceship to return home.
But survival is far from easy—the odds are always against you.

#🎮 Core Gameplay Features

#🔨 Resource Mining

Dig into the Moon’s terrain using an axe.

Mining takes 5 seconds per ore.

Upgrade your speed and mining cooldown to progress faster.

#🛒 Shop & Upgrades

Trade mined ores for money.

Purchase spaceship parts like:

Thrusters

Body

Base

Nose

Once purchased, parts never reset, even after death.

#👽 Alien Invasions

Every 3 minutes, aliens invade the Moon.

You cannot fight back—only dodge attacks.

The shop is the only safe zone.

#❤️ Life & Reset System

You have 3 lives.

Each hit reduces 1 life.

Life reaching 0 resets your cash but restores you to 3 lives.

#🧭 Controls

Move: WASD / Arrow Keys

Mine: Hold E + Movement Key

#🎯 Theme
“The Odds Are Against You.”

A game about endurance, pressure, and survival in an endless loop meant to challenge your determination.
